Overviews

I proposed a research plan and it was accepted by National College Student Research Scholarship. This project involved a one-year based experiment, focusing on helping teachers to develop interactive teaching materials of ecology through the technology of Augmented Reality. Children using the material could enjoy interesting interaction as well as understand the development of different ecosystems. Though the focus of the research is on the application of Augmented Reality, but it also helped to learn more about the importance of illustration. By learning the maturing process of butterflies.

My Objectivess

  • The interactive game design can not only improve the interest, but also give attention to the learning function.
  • Assisting students with learning difficulties or inattention to improve their interest in learning
  • Assisting teachers as the medium of teaching materials can carry out a more complete AR interactive system.

The Project Plan

For the butterfly ecological growth story, P. thaiwanus Buttlerfly was used for research, and I also went to the Nature Ecological Museum for exploration. And that grow ecological habit is firstly designed and present in a conceptual diagram so as to be convenient to understand the progress of each stage process
