Search Menu Redesign

Overviews

Rakuya is a network service company jointly established by PChome Online(Taiwan B2B) .it mainly provides property for renting/selling online platform. We are committed to letting users have a good experience platform for our agents and renters.

In this project, we focus on optimising mobile devices. The original one made users have lots of bad experiences because our design team had a bad discussion at that moment. But we will still face the problem squarely and explore the real situation through various research methods. It turns out we fixed it and gained great positive feedback in the end.

Our Approach

My contribution

As a UX Developer, I participate in competitive product analysis, empathy analysis, and feedback. Another colleague and I are responsible for determining the most feasible method to measure time and execution costs. I give two suggestions. 1. The premise of not changing too much static architecture. 2. Added the most imporant elements for users to the second design version. And I handle drawing wireframe and front-end static cutting.

Empathy map+Competitive analysis

We reviewed the interface issues through the empathy experience to discover the problems with the first version of the search panel.
task1: 我想在台北市大安區尋找4000-5000萬 4房 無車位
task2: 我想在苗栗縣竹南鎮尋找1000-3000萬 透天 屋齡20-30年 3房

We sort out some key points from 1. See, 2. Do, 3. Think, and 4. feel . These four sides to discussion our experiences.There a two points.
1. The unfriendly design of the menu causes the user to repeatedly get stuck in the back and forth operation of the next step.
2. Users feel frustrated about the nothing result regularly.

We surveyed again to find out the process of competitive products which in same tasks(task1&2), and found that we took 5 steps more than the other competitive products. Besides, keep good features on these products.

Collecteced user’s feedback

We had two strongly feedback from agencies who collected by our customer dept. And also from general users who had related issues too.But, The feedback verifies our research result.

Customers and agents gave us excellent feedback afterwards. After solving these pain points, our future customers have grown a lot, and it has driven our company. The turnover has more than doubled growth, bringing in profits.
